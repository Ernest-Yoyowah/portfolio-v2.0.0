ABOUT ME

I am a Software Engineer based in Accra, Ghana, with a focus on building
frontend systems for payment products and digital platforms. Over the past
several years I have grown from writing my first production website to leading
frontend engineering on products used by hundreds of thousands of people across
Ghana. My work sits at the intersection of user-facing interfaces and critical
financial infrastructure — a space that demands precision, security consciousness,
and a deep respect for the people on the other side of every transaction.

───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

MY JOURNEY

I started my career in 2020 at DMI (Dkeys Music Institute), where I led the
development of their official website. It was my first real taste of building
something for a real audience — applying modern UI/UX principles, implementing
responsive design, and thinking about SEO and brand presence from the ground up.
The stack was straightforward (ReactJS, HTML5, CSS3), but the experience taught
me how to align technical work with business and communication goals, and how
to collaborate with stakeholders who do not speak in code.

In mid-2023 I joined Xcelsz as an Agile Developer for a short but formative
engagement. I worked within a cross-functional team on improving mobile
responsiveness across their web products — analysing the existing interface,
identifying responsiveness gaps, and implementing solutions using CSS media
queries and responsive design patterns. The Agile environment sharpened my
ability to work iteratively, communicate progress, and ship incrementally.

That same year — July 2023 — I joined the MTN PULSE Development Team, working
through Dexwin Tech. This is where my career shifted significantly. I was placed
on the MTN Pulse app, MTN's youth-focused platform serving over 100,000 active
users. When I arrived, the codebase had accumulated serious architectural debt.
I conducted a thorough analysis of the existing system, identified over 15
critical inefficiencies, and developed a migration plan that reduced technical
debt by 40%. The result was a more scalable, maintainable, and performant
application that gave the team the headroom to move faster and build with more
confidence.

In parallel with that work I was also involved in frontend integration for
MTN's payment products — building user-facing flows for Mobile Money
transactions, PCI DSS-compliant Mastercard hosted checkout, and airtime top-up.
I implemented OTP verification flows, SIM swap validation, and Ghana Card (NIA)
integration for high-risk operations. I take security on the frontend seriously:
OWASP-aligned input sanitisation, secure token handling, safe redirects, and
no sensitive card data ever touching our frontend — those are not optional
considerations, they are the baseline.

In March 2025 I was promoted to Lead Frontend Engineer, a recognition of the
work I had put in and the trust the team placed in me.

Between June and November 2024 I also worked at Jadaad Technologies as a
Software Engineer, contributing to Hygeieiora and Jadaad web applications.
I built and maintained reusable component libraries using React, TypeScript,
Material-UI, and Tailwind CSS, integrated backend APIs, and collaborated closely
with designers to ship user-centric interfaces on time.

WHAT I BUILD

My work spans several interconnected areas:

Payment & Fintech Systems
I build the frontend layer for payment products — the screens, flows, and
interactions that users rely on when moving money. That includes transaction
flows (MSISDN input, payment method selection, OTP verification, status polling),
resilient error handling for duplicate submissions and asynchronous payment
states, and compliance-aligned implementations for PCI DSS and OWASP. I am
careful and methodical in this space because mistakes cost people money and
erode trust.

Products I have contributed to include:
- MTN Pay — MTN's central payment gateway handling Mobile Money, Mastercard
  hosted checkout, and airtime across multiple MTN services
- MTN Self Service Portal (SSP) — customer-facing platform for account
  management, broadband, SIM/eSIM provisioning, and in-portal payments
- MTN Pulse — youth platform within the MyMTN app
- MTN Auth Services — Auth0-based centralised authentication consumed by
  multiple MTN applications in Ghana and South Africa

GPS & Fleet Tracking
I led the full-stack redevelopment of the Autosurveil GPS platform — a fleet
intelligence system with real-time vehicle monitoring, route analytics, and
OTP-based authentication flows. I owned both the frontend (Next.js, TypeScript)
and backend (Node.js, MongoDB) for that project.

Music Technology
Outside of my primary engineering work I build tools for musicians. PulseMIDI
is a native macOS MIDI diagnostic and routing tool I am building with Rust,
Tauri, and React — it talks directly to CoreMIDI and provides real-time signal
visualisation and device management. PulsePad is a performance pad controller
system for live musicians, currently in active development. These projects exist
because I am a musician as well as an engineer, and I want to solve problems I
actually experience.
